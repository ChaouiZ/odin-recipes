Sweet and Spicy Pork Bowl

A deliciously sweet and mildly spicy pork loin over rice with pickled veggies.

These spicy honey-glazed pork rice bowls with tangy pickled carrots and cucumber are a perfect healthy dinner.

Makes two servings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 - 1 1/2 lb. pork loin, cut into 3-inch strips
  • 1 small carrot, julienne cut
  • 1 seedless cucumber, julienne cut
  • 6 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • 2-3 inches of ginger root, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup of honey
  • 1/4 cup of chili garlic sauce
  • 1 bunch of cilantro, rough chopped
  • 3 tbsp. rice wine vinegar
  • 3 tbsp. of neutral cooking oil
  • 1/2 cup of green onion, chopped
  • Optional: Mint, lime, and sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat a pan over medium-high heat, add 1 tbsp. of oil
  2. Mix carrots, cucumbers and cilantro in a bowl with 2 tbsp. vinegar
  3. Mix together honey, chili garlic sauce, and 1 tbsp. vinegar
  4. Boil rice in 2 cups of water and a pinch of salt
    Hint: a rice cooker makes this step much easier
  5. Cook pork until brown on all sides
    This may require cooking in multiple batches. Add oil as needed
  6. Remove pork and set aside
  7. Add remaining oil along with garlic and ginger, brown for 30 seconds or until fragrant
  8. Deglaze the pan with sauce mixture, let simmer for 2 minutes or until it reaches desired thickness
    Stir often to avoid burning
  9. Add pork and its juices back to pan, coat well with the mixture, and remove from heat
  10. Serve pork over rice
  11. Garnish with pickled veggies, green onion, cilantro, mint and lime
  12. Enjoy!
