Sweet and Spicy Pork Bowl

A deliciously sweet and mildly spicy pork loin over rice with pickled veggies.

These spicy honey-glazed pork rice bowls with tangy pickled carrots and cucumber is a perfect healthy dinner.

Makes two servings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 - 1 1/2 lb. pork loin, cut into 3-inch strips
  • 1 small carrot, julienne cut
  • 1 seedless cucumber, julienne cut
  • 6 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • 2-3 inches of ginger root, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup of honey
  • 1/4 cup of chili garlic sauce
  • 1 bunch of cilantro, rough chopped
  • 3 tbsp. rice wine vinegar
  • 3 tbsp. of neutral cooking oil
  • 1/2 cup of green onion, chopped
  • Optional: Mint and sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat, add 1 tbsp. of oil
