Sex in the Driveway

A fun and fruity summer cocktail perfect for summer cookouts.

This crowd-pleasing cocktail is easy to make in large batches for a quick punch bowl.

Recipe makes one serving, but just increase the amounts for a party-sized pitcher!

Ingredients:

  • 1 oz. Blue Curacao
  • 1 oz. Peach Schnapps
  • 2 oz. Vodka
  • 6 oz. Sprite
  • Ice
  • Optional: Lemon slice for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Add ice to a glass
  2. Pour in Curacao, Schnapps, Vodka, and Sprite
  3. Garnish with lemon slice
  4. Get drunk!
